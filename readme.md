## üéØ About the Course

**Bioinformatics Ka Chilla** is an intensive one-year online course designed for students, professionals, and aspiring bioinformaticians who want to harness the power of biological data for scientific research and real-world applications.

### What You'll Learn

- **Basic to Advanced Bioinformatics**: Introduction to biological data, databases, and tools
- **Data Science for Life Scientists**: Machine learning, statistics, and data mining applied to biology
- **Practical Skills**: Hands-on experience with real-world datasets (genomics, pangenomics, metagenomics, proteomics, metabolomics, etc.)
- **Data Visualization**: Create impactful charts, graphs, and dashboards for biological data
- **Ethics in Bioinformatics**: Understand ethical, legal, and social implications of biomedical data
- **Tool Development**: Build and deploy your own bioinformatics tools

### Course Details

- **Mode**: Online with Learning Management System (LMS)
- **Language**: Urdu/Hindi and English
- **Duration**: 1 Year (Nov 1, 2025 - Oct 31, 2026)
- **Time Commitment**: 2-4 hours daily
- **Assessments**: Monthly quizzes, final project, and live workshops
- **Certification**: Yes, upon completion of assignments and final project
